About ASTRAVANA

ASTRAVANA is a grassroots wildlife conservation initiative led by Anshu Giri, known as The Bihar Guy. Operating across forests, wetlands, and communities, it focuses on anti-poaching operations, wildlife rescue, youth engagement, and public awareness programs throughout Bihar. The mission is to create a sustainable conservation culture by transforming hunters and local communities into protectors of wildlife.

Anshu Giri | Nature Conservationist

Anshu Giri has spent over three years actively protecting wildlife in Bihar. He has conducted 20+ anti-poaching raids, rescued dozens of species including foxes, owls, nilgai, and porcupine, and implemented community awareness programs across schools and colleges. His most famous project involves foxes rehabilitated across three generations, continuously thriving in their natural habitat with active youth supervision. Anshu’s work emphasizes education, engagement, and sustainable conservation practices.
