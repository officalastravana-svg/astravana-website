Our Vision

Astra Vana Foundation envisions a Bihar where wildlife thrives without fear, illegal hunting is eliminated, and communities actively participate in protecting biodiversity. Our mission is to build a long-term conservation movement rooted in awareness, action, and rehabilitation.

Field Story & Impact

For over 3+ years, our ground operations have been active across Bihar, including conservation efforts around Baba Keval Sthan and nearby rural ecosystems.

We have successfully rehabilitated and protected numerous wildlife species, including owls, foxes, migratory birds, and other vulnerable animals affected by illegal hunting and habitat loss.

Our field-based model focuses on direct rescue operations, public awareness drives, and youth engagement to create long-term conservation impact.
