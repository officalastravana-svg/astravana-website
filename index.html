About ASTRAVANA

ASTRAVANA is a grassroots wildlife conservation initiative led by Anshu Giri. It focuses on anti-poaching operations, wildlife rescue, youth engagement, and public awareness programs across Bihar. The mission is to create a sustainable conservation culture by transforming hunters and communities into wildlife protectors.

Anshu Giri | Nature Conservationist

Anshu Giri has spent over three years actively protecting wildlife in Bihar. He has conducted 20+ anti-poaching raids, rescued dozens of species including foxes, owls, nilgai, and porcupine, and implemented community awareness programs across schools and colleges. His most famous project involves foxes rehabilitated across three generations, continuously thriving in their natural habitat with active youth supervision.
